# CheddarBooks LaundryLog Introduction

LaundryLog is a small tool app under the CheddarBooks domain and branding area.

LaundryLog is meant for a very ordinary real-life problem:

you spend money doing laundry, often away from home, and later you want a trustworthy record of what you spent.

That might mean:

- a truck driver doing laundry at a truck stop
- someone using a laundromat while traveling
- a household keeping track of recurring laundry costs
- anyone who wants a simple record when receipts are missing, awkward, or easy to lose

## The Problem Space

Laundry expenses are usually small, repetitive, and easy to forget.

You may pay for:

- washers
- dryers
- detergent or other supplies

Those costs often happen in a setting where:

- you are in a hurry
- you are carrying clothes or supplies
- you are using a phone, not sitting at a desk
- you may not get a good receipt
- you may not want to deal with a complicated bookkeeping app

By the time you need the information later, the details can be fuzzy.

## The Scenario LaundryLog Is For

LaundryLog is for the moment when you are actually doing laundry and want to capture what happened while it is still fresh.

A typical scenario looks like this:

1. you arrive at a location
2. you start a laundry outing or session
3. you log one or more expenses as they happen
4. you keep a running sense of what the total has become
5. later, you still have a usable record even if the receipt is gone

## What The App Should Feel Like

LaundryLog should feel:

- quick
- simple
- phone-friendly
- understandable without training
- useful even when offline

It is not meant to feel like full accounting software, even though it now sits under the broader CheddarBooks brand area.

## What The App Helps A User Do

LaundryLog helps a user:

- remember where the expense happened
- record what kind of laundry expense it was
- capture quantity and price
- see the running total for one outing
- keep enough detail for later recordkeeping, reimbursement, or tax-related use

## What It Is Not Trying To Be

LaundryLog is not trying to be:

- a giant finance suite
- a complex business system
- a social app
- a replacement for every bookkeeping workflow

It is a small focused tool for one recurring real-world need.

## Why It Matters

For some people, laundry is just a minor household chore.

For others, especially people who live or work on the road, it is a real recurring expense that deserves a better record than memory alone.

LaundryLog exists to make that record easy to keep.
